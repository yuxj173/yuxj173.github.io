Some practical things for win10?

Posted on 2016-05-25   |  

This will be the last post at least until June. Recent several days I did some configuration for my win10, and it seems not everyone knows that win10 has some really handy shortcuts and features, at least one of my friends did…So I then will show three frequently used.

The Sidebar

The programs pinned to sidebar then are able to be called by win+shift+number, and win+number is used to switch between them.

Console

In explorer, alt+d will activate the address bar, and then simply type ‘cmd’ will call the console.

How to call it as an administrator? Try typing F10,f,m,a one after another.

AutoHotKey

Extremely useful.

For example, remapping 1,2,3,q,w,e,a,d to 1-9 just like the keypad to facilitate sidebar-switching. Or make a script to call the console.
